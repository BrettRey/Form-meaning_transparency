## What it is

The form-meaning arm of the mediated-accessibility programme: three within-domain
windows on form-meaning transparency (constructional compositionality, compound
transparency, masked priming) and the cross-domain cluster question, whether the
three are joint reflexes of a single underlying organization. The cross-modal probe
(currently `D1-appendix-d.tex`) is the intended empirical spine.
